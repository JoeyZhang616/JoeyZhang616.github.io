About Me

Hello! I am Fafa Zhang, a Master's student in Artificial Intelligence at Anhui University. My research interests lie in large language models, reinforcement learning, and decision optimization, with a focus on bridging LLM reasoning capabilities with structured planning and search methods.

I am affiliated with the Anhui Provincial Key Laboratory of Security Artificial Intelligence, advised by Prof. Hui Wang. I have also interned as an AI Algorithm Engineer at Conch Group (Fortune 500), where I worked on deploying and fine-tuning large language models for industrial applications, including model serving optimization, prompt engineering, and downstream task adaptation.
